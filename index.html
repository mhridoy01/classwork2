FIFA World Cup 2022 News:

The biggest score line in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers - and indeed in the history of
international football – was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0.

This legendary match also brought global renown for Article Thompson, whose 13-goal haul set a new
world record, which stands to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.

And though the defeat earned American Samoa ignominy, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been
that it is now the subject of an acclaimed documentary, ‘Next Goal Wins’, showing across the world.
